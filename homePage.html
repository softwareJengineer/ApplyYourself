Our Mission

Welcome to Apply Yourself, or, as I like to call it, Discount NavianceTM.


As rising juniors and seniors, the college process looms as a daunting obstacle. Trying to frantically gather information before deadlines induces anxiety in students, and, due to financial restraints, not all students are able to easily acquire this information. We at Apply Yourself aim to alleviate this stress by providing a free, simple, and easy way to access necessary resources to help students thrive.



Apply Yourself offers a variety of tools to assist in the college process. The College Counselor page includes an easy-to-use table for information and a graph for visual learners. Our resources page allows students to access a calendar of many popular schools' application deadlines and articles that students may find helpful.
