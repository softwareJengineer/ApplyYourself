Pamela Cameron

Co founder of Apply Yourself

Rising junior at the Latin School of Chicago.

I enjoy singing, acting, reading, and playing tennis. Apply yourself tries to close the gap in the college application process, as there are some students who do not have access to applications like naviance or a college counselor who is willing to take the time to meet the student. This is mostly because of a financial problem as naviance can be quite expensive for some school districts. We wanted all students to have the same opportunity to understand the college process without financial burdens, so we created apply yourself!
